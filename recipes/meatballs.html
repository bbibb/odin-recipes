Italian Meatballs

Description

This is a basic and delicious recipe for Italian meatballs. They are tender and flavorful, and wonderful on pasta or on a meatball sub!

The recipe here uses ground beef, but you may also incorporate ground pork or ground turkey as you like. Also, I prefer to bake the meatballs and bag them for later use. However, you can also cook a small batch right within your pasta sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Ground beef
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 cup milk
  • 1 cup Italian style breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Combine breadcrumbs and milk in a small bowl, set aside for 5 minutes.
  3. Finely chop/mince onion and garlic.
  4. Beat eggs.
  5. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
  6. Gently mix ingredients with your hands until there are no pockets of single ingredients. Do not overmix; the meat mixture will be uneven.
  7. Gently form 2-inch balls, lightly rolling them in your palm to achieve a round shape.
  8. Place meatballs in a large sheet pan and bake 15-20 minutes, to an internal temperature of 160F. Do not overbake!
  9. Immediately transfer to a plate for cooling. They can be eaten now or bagged and frozen for later.
